Mission

Building bridges in the digital realm with trusted Organizational Credentials. Through vLEIs and the KERI ecosystem, we're fostering a collaborative landscape where every entity, big or small, can be verified with confidence. We envision a world where every business identity is authentic and protected.

Private Sector:

  • Navigating the intricate maze of modern digital identity tools? From understanding the ins and outs of vLEIs to harnessing the power of KERI, we're your compass in this complex journey.
  • As technology evolves, so should your business's identity solutions. Don't get left behind with outdated methods. With our forward-thinking approach, we ensure you're always ahead of the curve.
  • Struggling to manage multiple organizational identities across various platforms? We're creating a universal language for business identity that speaks across borders and systems.
  • In an increasingly digital world, how can you be sure the organization you're dealing with is who they say they are? Trust has become a premium commodity. We're here to help businesses establish and maintain that trust.

Public Sector:

  • In a world dominated by digital transactions and identities, ensuring the authenticity of organizations becomes a matter of national and economic security. Our solutions ensure that trust in the digital realm translates to security in the real world.
  • Engaging in international trade requires a bedrock of trust and verification. How can nations ensure the authenticity of entities across borders? We're paving the way for seamless, verified international business relationships.
  • Misidentification, fraud, and inefficiencies in verifying organizations can cost economies billions. We're here to drive efficiency, cut down redundancies, and boost the economic engine.
  • Financial systems that cross borders require a universally accepted and robust system of identity verification. Dive into the world of vLEIs with us and fortify the global financial infrastructure.
