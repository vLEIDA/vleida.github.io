Mission

Our mission is to empower businesses and institutions with the trust and transparency they need to thrive in the digital age. We are dedicated to revolutionizing the landscape of organizational identification and verifiability through cutting-edge technology, making it easier for legal entities to navigate the global market securely and efficiently. By pioneering innovative solutions in LEIs, vLEIs, and KERI, we aim to create a world where digital trust is seamless, accessible, and universally recognized, fostering economic growth, compliance, and integrity in the business ecosystem.

Legal Entity Identifier (LEI) Services:

Our LEI Services offer comprehensive solutions to help businesses navigate the world of Legal Entity Identifiers (LEIs) effectively. Whether it's regulatory compliance, risk management, or optimizing business processes, our consulting expertise ensures that your organization maximizes the benefits of LEIs. We assist in obtaining, renewing, and managing LEIs, while also providing guidance on integrating LEIs into your systems for enhanced efficiency. Our services encompass counterparty verification, supply chain risk management, data standardization, and technology integration. We also offer education and training programs to empower your team with the knowledge needed to harness the power of LEIs in the ever-evolving global business landscape.

verifiable Legal Entity Identifier (vLEI) Services:

Our vLEI Services are designed to unlock the potential of verifiable Legal Entity Identifiers (vLEIs) for your organization. With a focus on compliance, security, and digital trust, we provide comprehensive consulting solutions to help businesses fully leverage vLEIs. We guide you through the vLEI acquisition process with trusted QVIs, ensuring the secure and accurate management of your entity's credentials. We assist in integrating vLEIs into your operations, enhancing regulatory compliance, and streamlining processes. Our experts offer guidance on using vLEIs for identity verification, risk management, and facilitating secure digital transactions. Explore the future of organizational digital identity with our vLEI Services.

Key Event Receipt Infrastructure (KERI):

Our KERI Technical Services offer innovative solutions for organizations looking to leverage the Key Event Receipt Infrastructure (KERI). We provide expert guidance on KERI technology, including system design, implementation, and integration. Our services are designed to enhance data security and integrity, ensuring organizations can benefit from tamper-proof data exchange and event management. Explore the potential of KERI technology with our technical services, tailored to meet your unique needs.
