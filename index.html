vLEIDA

Mission

We like to make products for modern decentralized digital identity ecosystems, particularly aimed at the enterprise and the public sector. So called, organizational credentials. In particular we specialize in digital identity and security products revolving around the distributed public key infrastructure called KERI and its closely related deployment, the governance ecosystem of the verifiable Legal Entity Identifier (vLEI) and the Legal Entity Identifier (LEI) from the Global Legal Entity Identifier Foundation.
