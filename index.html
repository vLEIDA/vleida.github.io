Mission

We like to make products for modern decentralized digital identity ecosystems, particularly for the enterprise and the public sector. Recently, these ecosystems have come to be called, organizational credentials. In particular, we specialize in digital identity and security products revolving around the state of the art, distributed public key infrastructure called KERI and its closely related deployment, the governance ecosystem of the verifiable Legal Entity Identifier (vLEI). A brand new project building off of the work of the Legal Entity Identifier (LEI) from the Global Legal Entity Identifier Foundation (GLEIF).

You may be wondering, what are organizational credentials? Organizational credentials are a blanket term in identity circles that has come to define all the differing ways that a company or organization and agents of that company or organization identifies itself in real world interactions. In ancient times, a handshake and an accumulated reputation were all that a business person had to go on and today these two pillars are still the bedrock of commerce. However, the savvy and the quick are coming to understand that modern technology in a globalised world market can allow for reputational guarantees and communication of that reputation that their forebearers could only dream of. In a world increasingly fueled deep fakes, frauds, and now generative AI, digital organizational credentials are poised to take a more pivotal role.
