Mission

We like to make products to be used in modern decentralized digital identity ecosystems, particularly aimed at the enterprise and the public sector. So called, organizational credentials. In particular we specialize in digital identity and security products revolving around the brand new distributed public key infrastructure called KERI and its related, governance ecosystem of the vLEI and LEI from GLEIF.
